About Me

I am a student at Qibao Dwight High School with a deep passion for economics, sports management, rowing, and creative media. I enjoy tackling challenges from multiple perspectives—whether that means solving complex mathematics problems, pushing my physical limits during training, or producing engaging videos through editing and storytelling. These experiences have taught me the importance of curiosity, discipline, and continuous self-improvement.


Looking ahead, I hope to combine my interests in sports and business by exploring how economics, management, and innovation can shape the future of athletics. At the same time, I plan to continue developing as a video creator, using digital media to share stories, document experiences, and inspire others through both competition and creativity.

Background & Activities

Rowing has become one of the most meaningful parts of my life. Through years of training, I have learned that success is built on consistency, teamwork, and determination. I have competed in prestigious events such as the Head of the Charles Regatta (HOCR) and achieved strong performances in rowing competitions in Hong Kong. Each race has helped me become a stronger athlete while teaching me valuable lessons about perseverance, discipline, and leadership.


Today, I continue to train with the goal of competing at an even higher level, including preparation for the upcoming Shanghai Municipal Games. Beyond racing, I enjoy documenting my journey through photography and video production, sharing both the excitement of competition and the stories behind the hard work.
